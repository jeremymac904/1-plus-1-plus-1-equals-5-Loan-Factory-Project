## Purpose

Serve Spanish-speaking homebuyers, refinancers, and Realtor partners across all U.S. markets with a unified Loan Factory team marketing identity. Language-anchored, distinct from the Latino division (which is community-anchored).

## Audience

- First-time Spanish-speaking homebuyers
- Spanish-language Realtor partners
- Refinance candidates in Spanish-dominant households
- Bilingual households where Spanish is the primary financial-decision language

## Recruiting value

Spanish is the largest non-English language market in U.S. mortgage. A Spanish division gives bilingual LOs a publishable brand in every metro with a meaningful Spanish-speaking population.

## Marketing value

Shared Spanish-language content (newsletter, social, GBP, Realtor outreach), shared Realtor pool, simpler compliance through template-level bilingual review, broad market reach.
