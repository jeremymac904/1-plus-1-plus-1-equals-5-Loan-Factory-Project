# Latino Division (Placeholder)

**Status:** Early expansion track (Thuan-directed)
**Owner:** Jeremy McDonald (LO Dev) + future Team Leader
**Branding lead:** Marketing pod (post Team Leader confirmation)

## Purpose

Serve Latino-identifying homebuyers, refinancers, and Realtor partners with a unified Loan Factory team marketing identity. Community-anchored (cultural identity), distinct from the Spanish division (language-anchored).

## Audience

- Latino-identifying first-time homebuyers across the U.S.
- English-dominant, Spanish-dominant, and bilingual households
- Latino Realtor and partner network
- Multi-generational Latino households (cosigners, family purchases)
- Latino business owners (self-employed borrower content)

## Recruiting value

Latino LOs may not all be Spanish-fluent, but they share community identity, cultural fluency, and partner networks. A Latino division gives them a brand to operate behind even when content is in English.

## Marketing value

Shared community-anchored content (English, Spanish, or bilingual), shared Realtor pool, cultural positioning, partnerships with Latino business chambers and community organizations.
