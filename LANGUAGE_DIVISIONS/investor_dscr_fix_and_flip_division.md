## Purpose

Serve real estate investors, DSCR borrowers, fix-and-flip operators, and BRRRR-strategy investors with a unified Loan Factory team marketing identity focused on investment-property financing.

## Audience

- Real estate investors (first-time and seasoned)
- DSCR borrowers
- Fix-and-flip operators
- BRRRR-strategy investors
- Short-term rental (STR) and mid-term rental (MTR) operators
- Investor-focused Realtor partners
- Wholesale partners and operators in the investor network

## Recruiting value

Investor lending is a clear differentiator. LOs with non-QM, DSCR, fix-and-flip, and bridge experience bring expertise this division can amplify. Recruits LOs who feel under-leveraged in retail-only environments.

## Marketing value

Shared investor educational content, shared deal-structure explainers, BRRRR strategy content, DSCR qualification explainers, partnerships with investor meetups, fix-and-flip Realtor outreach, podcast and YouTube content with high investor appeal.
