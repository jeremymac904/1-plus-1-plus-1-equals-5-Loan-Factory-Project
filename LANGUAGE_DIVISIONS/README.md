# LANGUAGE_DIVISIONS

Per-division operating docs: Spanish/Latino, Vietnamese, Russian, Korean, Veteran/Military, Investor/DSCR, and future expansion.

## What goes here

- One doc per division (e.g., `spanish_latino.md`, `vietnamese.md`, `veteran_military.md`)
- Division-level brand notes
- Compliance reviewer assignments per division
- Sub-team structures (if a division has multiple regional teams)
- Division-specific Realtor partner pool patterns
- Division-specific recruiting notes

## What doesn't

- Individual team profiles (those live in `TEAM_LEADER_PROGRAM/`)
- Customer-facing in-language content (lives with the Marketing pod assets and ultimately the published channels)

## Naming

- Division doc: `[division_name].md` — all lowercase, underscores
- Example: `spanish_latino.md`, `vietnamese.md`, `russian_eastern_european.md`, `korean.md`, `veteran_military.md`, `investor_dscr.md`

## Division structure reference

These are umbrella growth communities and recruiting ecosystems inside Loan Factory. **Not separate companies.** Each can hold multiple sub-teams.

See the Drive execution folder's `LANGUAGE_AND_NICHE_DIVISIONS.md` for the canonical division definition.

## Future divisions

Future divisions (First-Time Buyer, HELOC, Self-Employed, Builder Partner, etc.) get added when:

1. A qualified Team Leader steps up
2. There's a clear underserved audience with referral potential
3. Marketing + TERA can absorb the additional team without dropping support for existing ones
