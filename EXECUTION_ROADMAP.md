**Goal:** Stand up the first wave. Get teams active. Capture friction.

- All first wave teams hit publishing cadence by Day 14
- First Pilot Team Leader meeting completed

**End-of-month checkpoint (June 30):**

- How many teams are active?
- Cadence compliance heat map?

- What's in the iteration queue for July?

## Critical path

If any of these slip, June 1 launch slips:

1. Tuesday leadership sign-off (May 19/20)
2. Marketing kit delivery (by May 29)
3. TERA channel setup capability confirmed (by May 22)
4. First wave Team Leader confirmations (by May 29)
5. Google Form intake live (by May 25)

Everything else is desirable. These five are required.
