# TEAM_LEADER_PROGRAM

Team Leader candidate tracking, pilot team profiles, scorecards, and the Team Leader role definition.

## What goes here

- First wave Team Leader candidate list (named, status, division)
- Pilot team profiles (one per active team)
- Team Leader scorecards (monthly, per team)
- Team Leader role definition (current and evolving)
- Team Leader comp design parking lot (Andre + Edward + Finance own the real work)

## What doesn't

- Individual LO information beyond the team roster (LO-level data lives in CRM)
- Customer / borrower information (never in repo)

## Naming

- Team profile: `[team_name]_profile.md`
- Team scorecard: `[team_name]_scorecard_YYYY-MM.md`
- Candidate list: `team_leader_candidates.md` (single file, updated continuously)

## Templates

- `TEMPLATE_PILOT_TEAM_PROFILE.md` for new team profiles
- `TEAM_LEADER_PROGRAM/TEMPLATE_TEAM_LEADER_SCORECARD.md` for monthly scorecards

## Privacy

NMLS numbers are fine to record here. Personal contact info (phone, personal email) should be minimized — use Loan Factory work emails. Never record borrower data.

## Cross-references

- `LANGUAGE_DIVISIONS/` — division-level structure each team belongs to
- `FORMS_AND_ONBOARDING/` — onboarding checklists per team
- `REPORTING_AND_TRACKING/` — cadence rollups across teams
