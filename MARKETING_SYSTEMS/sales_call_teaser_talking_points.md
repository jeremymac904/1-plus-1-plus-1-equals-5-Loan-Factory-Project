# Sales Call Teaser Talking Points

**Owner:** Jeremy McDonald
**Audience:** Loan Factory weekly sales call (all LOs)
**Length:** 45 seconds
**Goal:** Create interest. Drive Team Leaders to the next Team Leader meeting and to the May 25 intake form. Do not over-explain.
**Status:** Ready to deliver

## The teaser script

**Open (10 seconds).**
Quick one before we wrap. Loan Factory is rolling out a new marketing program built specifically for teams. It is called 1+1+1=5.

**The hook (15 seconds).**
Here is the math. One LO marketing alone gets one unit of reach. Three LOs together under one team brand, with shared content, shared partners, and shared infrastructure, get five or more. That is what we are unlocking.

**Who it is for (10 seconds).**
If you are leading a team, thinking about leading a team, or considering joining one, this is for you. This is an exclusive benefit of being on a Loan Factory team. If you are flying solo, this is the moment to find your three.

**The ask (10 seconds).**
More in the Team Leader meeting next week. Intake form goes live May 25. Anyone can raise their hand. Capacity-based waves start June 1. That is it. Talk soon.

## Slide on screen during the teaser

Single slide. Three lines only.

- **1 + 1 + 1 = 5**
- Team Marketing Enhancement Program
- Intake opens May 25, 2026

## Tone reminders

- Confident, conversational, real
- Not corporate, not hype
- High energy on the math beat
- Soft landing on the ask
- Do not list features
- Do not promise outcomes
- Do not reference comp design

## What to avoid

- Do not call it a "pilot" on the sales call. Open program, capacity-based waves.
- Do not name specific Team Leader candidates.
- Do not detail compliance guardrails (covered in the Team Leader meeting).
- Do not list every division. Mention divisions as a category if asked.

## If asked a question on the call

- "How do I apply?" Intake form goes live May 25. Anyone can raise a hand.
- "What does my team get?" A kit, channel setup, AI drafting, tracking, and a Marketing and TERA contact for the first 30 days. Full breakdown at the Team Leader meeting.
- "What if I am not on a team yet?" That is the point. This is an exclusive benefit of being on a team. Find three LOs or talk to me about division-based team formation.
- "When does Wave 1 launch?" June 1. Capacity-based prioritization.
