# Team Leader Meeting Talking Points

**Owner:** Jeremy McDonald
**Audience:** Loan Factory monthly Team Leader meeting
**Length:** 10 to 12 minutes (5-minute walkthrough, 5 to 7 minutes Q&A)
**Goal:** Explain the program clearly. Drive intake submissions. Surface concerns early.
**Status:** Ready to deliver

## 1. What this program is

1+1+1=5 is the Loan Factory Team Marketing Enhancement Program. It is an open program available to every Team Leader at Loan Factory. We give your team a unified brand, a reusable content kit, channel infrastructure across Ally, the portal, GBP, Email Drip, and Loan Factory IQ, and AI drafting support through Gemini and NotebookLM. We connect what Loan Factory already runs. We do not add new software. You bring the team. We bring the engine.

## 2. Who it is for

Any Team Leader at Loan Factory who has three or more LOs willing to operate under one brand, across at least two cities or states. Divisions function as umbrella identities, not separate teams.

Initial division umbrellas...
- Chinese
- Vietnamese
- Russian
- Spanish
- Korean
- Latino
- Veteran or Military
- Investor or DSCR or Fix and Flip

Regional and audience-based teams are also welcome. You can lead a team that operates inside one of those divisions or outside any of them.

## 3. How to apply

Open intake form goes live May 25. Anyone can raise a hand. Leadership prioritizes implementation order based on readiness, division fit, leadership priority, and Marketing and TERA capacity. Wave 1 begins June 1. Wave 2 and Wave 3 roll out as capacity opens. Being early helps. Not being early does not exclude you.

## 4. What you will receive

- A reusable team kit (templates for GBP posts, social, newsletters, Realtor outreach, Email Drip)
- A brand kit for your division (logos, colors, fonts)
- AI-drafted content through Gemini and NotebookLM, ready for human review
- Ally workflows
- A portal newsletter audience template
- Email Drip sequence
- A claimed GBP per market
- Template-level compliance approval (no waiting on per-piece review)
- Tracking visibility across all channels in the launch Google Sheet
- A named Marketing contact for your first 30 days
- A named TERA contact for platform issues

## 5. What accountability is required

Three things.

**Publishing cadence.** You hit the agreed posting, newsletter, and campaign cadence.

**Human review on every mortgage-related piece.** AI can draft, translate, summarize, speed up production. A human approves before publishing. No autopublish.

**Bi-weekly Pilot Team Leader meeting attendance.** I chair it. You surface friction. We fix it.

You also commit to monitoring. We track posting activity, newsletter activity, campaign activity, Team Leader participation, application growth, recruiting activity, and compliance review status. This is not punitive. It is how we learn fast and improve the program.

## 6. Why this is a recruiting advantage

A strong team brand pulls LOs in. The cost of recruiting drops. The team grows. Your shared content engine compounds. Your community trust compounds. This program is an exclusive benefit of being on a Loan Factory team. That means if you are an LO who wants this support, you either join an existing team or you form one. That is how we want it. That is how it should be.

## 7. Compliance guardrails

Loan Factory marketing policy is a hard rule, not a guideline. The guardrails are baked into the team kit templates so you are not memorizing them.

Required on applicable pieces...
- Marketing review when required
- Loan Factory name and NMLS 320841 disclosure
- LO NMLS disclosure
- Equal Housing Lender logo where required
- State-specific requirements
- Trigger term rules
- Rate and APR rules
- No misleading or unsupported claims
- No NPI
- $2,000 Best Price Guarantee terms in both caption and image or video when mentioned
- No $2,000 guarantee marketing in Washington state

Full checklist is in the kit. If you are not sure, route to Compliance before publishing. Better to be a day late than to fix it after it ships.

## 8. Next step

If you are interested, raise your hand on the intake form May 25. Bring questions to the next Team Leader meeting. Talk to the LOs around you. Start thinking about your team brand identity.

Open Q&A.

## Likely questions and answers

- **"Can we keep our existing team name and brand?"** Yes. Existing teams keep their identity and adopt whatever pieces of the kit help. The Legends Mortgage Team is an example.
- **"Do I have to be on a language division?"** No. Regional, audience-based, and undivided teams are welcome.
- **"Can an LO be on two teams?"** Yes, but they pick one primary for attribution. See PROJECT_DECISIONS_AND_CONTEXT.md D20.
- **"What about comp for Team Leaders?"** Comp design is parallel work with Andre, Edward, and Finance. First wave Team Leaders operate under current comp during launch. Comp model target lock: August 30 retro.
- **"What if my team only has two LOs right now?"** Three is the floor. Use the program announcement as your recruiting tool. Submit intake when you have three committed.
- **"What if I want to lead a team but I am not yet a Team Leader?"** Apply on the intake form. Mark "Aspiring Team Leader." We will talk through it.
- **"Can Marketing actually keep up?"** Marketing capacity gates wave size. We do not exceed what they can deliver. That is the discipline.

## Tone reminders

- Direct, confident, practical
- Anti-corporate
- Pro-broker, pro-Loan-Factory-team
- Warm to first-time Team Leaders
- Honest about what we do not yet know
