# Vision and Mission

## Vision

A Loan Factory where every loan officer has the brand, the platform, and the partner network of a top-1% producer — because they're operating inside a marketing team that gives them all three.

Language doesn't limit reach. Niche doesn't isolate. Geography doesn't cap growth. Every community Loan Factory serves has a team brand that's already trusted, already publishing, already recruiting.

## Mission

Connect Loan Factory's existing infrastructure into **repeatable, scalable team marketing kits.** Give Team Leaders a brand to operate behind, a content engine they don't have to build from scratch, and a partner pool they share with their team. Make the Marketing pod's leverage scale with the platform — not with headcount.

## What we believe

1. **Solo LO marketing is broken at scale.** The math doesn't work for a single LO to do paid ads, content, partner outreach, podcast production, social media, and lead nurture alone. The work belongs to a team.
2. **Existing systems beat new builds.** Ally, TERA, the portal, GBP — these tools work. We don't need to replace them. We need to connect them.
3. **AI drafts. Humans approve.** AI handles the production load. Humans hold compliance, judgment, and voice. No autopublish.
4. **Compliance is template-level.** Bake the rules into the asset, not into the review queue. The $2,000 Lowest Rate and Fee Guarantee disclosure lives in the template, not in someone's head.
5. **Team brands are recruiting flywheels.** A strong team brand pulls LOs in. The cost of recruiting drops as the brand strengthens.
6. **Capacity gates rollout.** We open as many teams as Marketing and TERA can actually support — not as many as we'd like to open.
7. **Friction surfaced fast is friction fixed fast.** The bi-weekly Pilot Team Leader meeting exists to surface what's broken before it compounds.

## What we value

- **Action over theory** — ship the kit, measure the friction, iterate the kit
- **Execution over presentation** — a working template beats a beautiful slide
- **Speed over perfection** — open June 1 with what we have, not with everything we want
- **Collaboration over hierarchy** — Team Leaders shape the framework alongside HQ
- **Existing tools over new builds** — Phase 1 is "make what we have actually work for teams"
- **Brand over personality** — the team brand outlasts any individual LO's social presence

## What we reject

- Bloated enterprise rollouts
- Consultant theater
- Vanity metrics
- KPI dashboards no one acts on
- Premature automation
- Comp restructures that hold up launches
- "We need one more month of planning"

## The 18-month picture

By the end of 2027, Loan Factory should have:

- 20–40 active marketing teams across language, niche, and regional divisions
- A Team Leader role with locked compensation and a clear career arc
- A standardized onboarding workflow that runs in 14 days, end-to-end
- Template-level compliance approval across all standard channels
- Cross-team content syndication (one team's win becomes everyone's win)
- AI-assisted Realtor outreach personalization in production
- Recruiting flywheel where strong team brands pull LOs in at a lower cost per hire
- An operational playbook good enough to license inside the broker channel

That's the long arc. Everything in this repo serves it.
