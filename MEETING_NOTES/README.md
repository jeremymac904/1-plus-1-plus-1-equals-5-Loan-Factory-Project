# MEETING_NOTES

Recap docs for every meeting that produces decisions or action items.

## What goes here

- Bi-weekly Pilot Team Leader meeting recaps
- Tuesday leadership meeting recaps
- Team Leader kickoff call notes (if substantive)
- Monthly leadership update copies
- 90-day pilot retro

## What doesn't

- Quick check-ins (use Google Chat)
- 1:1 conversations (unless they produced a decision)
- Marketing pod / TERA pod internal stand-ups

## Naming

`YYYY-MM-DD_short_name_of_meeting.md`

Example: `2026-06-05_first_pilot_leader_meeting.md`

## Template

Use `TEMPLATE_MEETING_NOTES.md` — copy it, rename, fill in.

## After every meeting

1. Save the recap here
2. Promote any new decisions to `PROJECT_DECISIONS_AND_CONTEXT.md`
3. Push action items to `NEXT_ACTIONS_QUEUE.md`
4. Tag the meeting in the next monthly leadership update if relevant
