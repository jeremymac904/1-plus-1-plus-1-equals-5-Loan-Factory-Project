## Failure modes to watch

If any of these surface, escalate to Jeremy.

- [ ] GBP not claimed by Day 5 — Team Leader needs to claim immediately
- [ ] LO count below 3 — recruitment pause until minimum met
- [ ] Team Leader can't commit time — escalate to consider reassignment
- [ ] Brand kit blocked on logo decisions past Day 5 — ship standard variant, iterate later
- [ ] Compliance reviewer unassigned past Day 7 — escalate to Compliance lead

## 30-day QA checkpoint

At Day 30, Jeremy reviews against:

- [ ] Hit cadence at least 3 of the 4 weeks
- [ ] First newsletter went out
- [ ] First Realtor outreach went out
- [ ] Team-level meeting happened at least once
- [ ] No outstanding TERA / Marketing tickets

If 2+ items missing, active intervention scheduled (not removal — intervention).
