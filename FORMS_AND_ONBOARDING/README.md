# FORMS_AND_ONBOARDING

The Google Form intake, onboarding checklists, and the operating docs for getting a new team from form → live.

## What goes here

- Google Form intake field definition
- Google Form intake link (when live)
- Onboarding checklist per team (use template)
- Onboarding workflow runbook (high-level workflow lives in `WORKFLOWS/`)
- Pre-launch email template for first wave Team Leaders

## What doesn't

- Team profiles (those live in `TEAM_LEADER_PROGRAM/`)
- Workflow definition (lives in `WORKFLOWS/`)

## Naming

- Onboarding checklist: `[team_name]_onboarding.md`
- Form definition: `google_form_intake_fields.md`
- Pre-launch email: `pre_launch_email_template.md`

## Template

Use `TEMPLATE_ONBOARDING_CHECKLIST.md` for new team onboarding.

## End-to-end timeline

Two weeks from form submission to live (Day 0 → Day 12). See `EXECUTION_ROADMAP.md` and the Drive execution folder's `TEAM_LEADER_ONBOARDING_WORKFLOW.md`.

## Cross-references

- `WORKFLOWS/team_leader_onboarding.md` — workflow steps
- `TEAM_LEADER_PROGRAM/` — team profiles that result from onboarding
- `MARKETING_SYSTEMS/` — kit delivered during onboarding
- `TERA_AND_TECH_REQUIREMENTS/` — channels stood up during onboarding
