# Team Leader Intake Form Fields

**Owner:** Jeremy McDonald (with Marketing review)
**Status:** Final field list, ready to build in Google Forms
**Target live date:** May 25, 2026
**Form name:** `1+1+1=5 Team Marketing Enhancement Program — Team Leader Intake`

## Purpose

Open intake form for any Loan Factory Team Leader (current, aspiring, or LO interested in joining a team) to raise a hand for the Team Marketing Enhancement Program. Submission does not guarantee Wave 1 placement. Implementation is capacity-based.

## Form behavior

- Hosted in Google Forms.
- Results auto-route to a Google Sheet (`1+1+1=5 Intake Submissions`).
- Notification on submission to Jeremy, Tara, Marketing pod lead, TERA pod lead.
- Review SLA: 24 to 48 hours.
- Open intake stays live continuously, not just for Wave 1.

## Form sections and fields

### Section 1. Applicant

1. Full name (short answer, required)
2. NMLS number (short answer, required)
3. Loan Factory email (short answer, required, email validation)
4. Phone (short answer, required)
5. Primary city and state (short answer, required)
6. Are you currently a Team Leader, applying to become one, or an LO joining a team? (multiple choice, required)
   - Current Team Leader
   - Aspiring Team Leader
   - LO joining a team

### Section 2. Team

7. Proposed or existing team name (short answer, required)
8. Existing Loan Factory team or new team? (multiple choice, required)
   - Existing Loan Factory team
   - New team being formed
9. Division umbrella (checkboxes, can pick more than one, required)
   - Chinese
   - Vietnamese
   - Russian
   - Spanish
   - Korean
   - Latino
   - Veteran or Military
   - Investor or DSCR or Fix and Flip
   - Regional (specify in next field)
   - Audience-based (specify in next field)
   - None or Other
10. If Regional, Audience, or Other, describe (short answer, optional)
11. Number of LOs currently committed to the team (short answer, required, minimum 3 for Wave consideration)
12. Are committed LOs across at least 2 cities or states? (multiple choice, required)
    - Yes
    - No
    - Not yet
13. Names and NMLS numbers of committed LOs (long answer, required)

### Section 3. Readiness

14. What languages does your team operate in? (checkboxes)
    - English
    - Chinese (Mandarin)
    - Chinese (Cantonese)
    - Vietnamese
    - Russian
    - Spanish
    - Korean
    - Other (specify)
15. Markets you cover (long answer, required)
16. Existing partner relationships (Realtors, builders, financial advisors, etc.) (long answer)
17. Current marketing activities your team is already doing (long answer)
18. Content you can produce consistently (checkboxes)
    - Social posts
    - Newsletters
    - Video
    - Podcast
    - GBP posts
    - Realtor outreach
    - Community events
    - None yet
19. Estimated publishing cadence you can commit to (multiple choice, required)
    - Daily
    - 3 to 5 times per week
    - 1 to 2 times per week
    - Monthly
    - Not sure yet

### Section 4. Why and what you need

20. Why do you want this for your team? (long answer, required)
21. What is your biggest marketing bottleneck right now? (long answer)
22. What support do you most need from Marketing and TERA? (long answer)
23. Anything else leadership should know (long answer, optional)

### Section 5. Acknowledgments

24. I understand human review is required on all mortgage-related content before publishing. (yes / no, required, yes to proceed)
25. I understand Loan Factory marketing policy applies, including NMLS, EHL, state-specific, trigger term, and $2,000 Best Price Guarantee rules. (yes / no, required, yes to proceed)
26. I understand implementation happens in capacity-based waves and submission does not guarantee Wave 1 placement. (yes / no, required, yes to proceed)
27. I understand I will attend the bi-weekly Pilot Team Leader meeting if implemented. (yes / no, required, yes to proceed)

### Section 6. Final

28. Best time to schedule a kickoff call if selected (short answer)
29. Signature (typed full name, short answer, required)
30. Date submitted (auto, hidden field)
